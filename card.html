Komodo Dragon

Komodo Dragon's can use their tongue to detect their prey by smell up to 4 miles away!

  • Scientific Name: Varanus komodoensis
  • Average Length:8.5 feet for males and 7.5 feet for females
  • Average Lifespan: About 30 years in the wild
  • Habitat: Lower forests and open grasslands on volcanic islands in Indonesia
  • Special Attack: Poisonous venom

While being named a dragon, believe it or not Komodo Dragons do not spit fire, nor can they fly. However, they are largest lizards on Earth reaching up to 10 feet and they can run up to 13 miles per hour. Females can also reproduce through both sexual and asexual reproduction.
